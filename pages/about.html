About Timothy.

“I don’t design trends. I design experiences.”

I’m Timothy Mokgolobotho, a self-taught designer, coder, and technician based in Polokwane, South Africa. Since 2021, I’ve been crafting bold digital experiences and fixing electronics with precision.

My journey has been shaped by passion, discipline, and the drive to blend clean UI design with powerful coding. Every project is a chance to push boundaries and tell a story through simplicity.

Blvck Aesthetix is more than a brand — it’s a digital lab where I showcase my skills, creativity, and relentless pursuit of excellence.
