About Me

Hi, I’m Timothy, the developer behind Blvck Aesthetix. I’m a self-taught full-stack developer who builds digital experiences from start to finish. I combine clean, interactive front-end design with solid backend architecture to create seamless, functional, and memorable projects.

I work with HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and Three.js for engaging interfaces, MongoDB and Firebase for database and server-side solutions, and Vercel for deployment and hosting. I also use Inkscape for SVG and design assets, ensuring every project looks as good as it performs.

Blvck Aesthetix is my personal lab—a space to experiment, learn, and push boundaries. Each project is an opportunity to blend design and code, exploring new ideas while delivering practical solutions. I thrive on solving challenges, refining workflows, and building experiences that are both visually striking and technically robust.

Skills & Tools Front-End: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Three.js, Responsive Design Back-End & Database: MongoDB, Firebase, Node.js, API Integration Design & UI/UX: Figma, Inkscape (SVG), Branding, Prototyping Deployment & Cloud: Vercel, Firebase Hosting Workflow & Tools: Git/GitHub, VS Code, Debugging & Optimization
