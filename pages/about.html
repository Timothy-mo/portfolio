About Me

Yo, I’m Timothy, the mind and hands behind Blvck Aesthetix. I’m a designer and technician blending raw energy with minimal execution. I build websites, design identities, and bring aesthetic pressure to screens, prints, and real-life builds.

I didn’t start with much. Just curiosity and the hunger to express what I couldn't afford. Everything I know, from design to code, came from late nights, failed attempts, and figuring things out on my phone and PC. No courses. No excuses. Just vibes, vision, and application.

Blvck Aesthetix isn’t a brand, it’s my lab. A personal movement to disrupt the ordinary. If it doesn’t hit different, it’s wasted space.
